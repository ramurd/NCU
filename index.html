It's time

It's time to choose you, you've been waiting and watching for too long, it's time for you to say to yourself "it's your turn". It's time to stand up to the woudla, coulda, shoulda family. You can do it. In fact, the woulda, coulda, shoulda family has way overstayed it's welcome. Their couch surfing has destroyed your familyroom, tv and home. It's time to stand up for yourself and demand that they leave. This is your life to live not theirs. It's time for them to move along. Don't worry there will be plenty of room for them somewhere else. You no longer have to think that you are the only one for them. They might put up a fight when when they leave. They may snarl and glare. This is the look of the future. This is you taking charge of you. Put away the past and focus on what you can do in the present to achieve your future goals.
